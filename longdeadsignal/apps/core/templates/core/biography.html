Band Biography

"Long Dead Signal are considered one of the local bands expected to step up another notch in 2011, and rightly so. Likening themselves to Buckley, Radiohead and Muse (and these comparisons, it has to be said, are spot on), their self-titled EP roars through six tracks from intricate guitars and distorted vocals at breakneck tempo, through to calmer melodies backed by inspired effects and riffs."
Ben does a lot of singing from the looks of it. In this photo he seems to be looking into the camera as if to say "hello". When he was 5 years old he fell into toxic liquid which gave him his super singing powers. He is never seen without his trademark scarf!
Gary handles percussion, and is the first member in the line up to wear glasses. Head tilted to the side, Gary always looks a life from a different angle.
Jaime handles the bass. When he's not laying down licks for Long Dead Signal, he (like Gary) enjoys wearing glasses. The only member to sport a tie and hat combo - which he has perfected over many years - Jaime leads the way with stylish poses, improving on Gary's already fantastic trademark side glance.
Jamie uses his scarf wearing capabilities to team up with Ben on vocals, whilst churning out riffs on the guitar. Jamie's scarf however is so potently aggressive during performances, that a special belt has been fasted to his jacket to keep it out of the way, and under control.
